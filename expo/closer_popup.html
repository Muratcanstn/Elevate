Closer

This artwork is made for the memories of my very lovely friends which I know for 6 years. We met during our first year of university at Clermont-Ferrand. We hang out very often and always together. We have been there for each others, helping, laughing, crying, drinking and eventually working (not as much as we should) together. And “Closer” from The Chainsmokers was our song, remembering us to stay as we were, close to each other.
